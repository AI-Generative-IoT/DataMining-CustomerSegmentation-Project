Unveiling Retail Dynamics: Mining Predictive Insights and Customer Segmentation from Online Retail Data  

This study investigates online retail dynamics by integrating customer segmentation and predictive analytics using the UCI Online Retail dataset. The primary objective is to identify behaviorally distinct customer groups and to accurately predict high-value customers (top 30% by revenue contribution) through a unified analytical pipeline.
